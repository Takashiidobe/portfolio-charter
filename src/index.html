Prices may be inaccurate due to smoothing/missing data. Do not base your financial decisions off of this.

You may use either stock tickers or cryptocurrency tickers. For stocks, just provide the ticker. For CryptoCurrencies, populate the ticker field with the ticker, followed by a dash, and then the currency code you'd like to compare it with. For example, to look at Bitcoin's USD price, enter `BTC-USD`.
